Safety First

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your fireworks PESO certified?

Yes. Every product we sell carries valid PESO (Petroleum and Explosives Safety Organisation) certification. We work only with licensed manufacturers and maintain rigorous quality control across our entire supply chain.

How do you ensure safe delivery?

All shipments are packaged in flame-retardant, shock-absorbing containers and transported via licensed carriers. We comply with all DGCA and road transport regulations for hazardous goods shipping.

Do you provide professional supervision for events?

Absolutely. Our trained pyrotechnicians travel to your event location and handle the complete setup, execution, and safety management of professional displays. We carry full event insurance.

Are eco-friendly options available?

Yes, we have a growing range of reduced-emission, low-noise fireworks using green chemistry. These products produce significantly less particulate matter while maintaining brilliant visual effects.

What is your return policy?

Unused, unopened items can be returned within 7 days of delivery. We offer full refunds or exchanges. For safety reasons, opened or partially used items cannot be returned.
